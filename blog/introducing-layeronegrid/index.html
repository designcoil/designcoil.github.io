Introducing LayerOneGrid: Managed Hosting Built for eCommerce

Over the years, one question has come up again and again from clients: "Where should we host this?" It sounds simple, but the answer rarely is. Generic shared hosting buckles under traffic spikes. Oversized cloud setups require a dedicated DevOps engineer to manage. And budget hosts make promises they can't keep when it actually matters.

So we built something better. Today we're launching LayerOneGrid - a managed hosting brand by DesignCoil, designed specifically for eCommerce stores and service businesses that depend on their website to generate revenue.

Why We Built It

After a decade of building and maintaining eCommerce sites on Magento, Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce, we know what good hosting needs to look like. It needs to be fast under real-world load. It needs to be stable without requiring constant attention. And when something goes wrong at 11pm before a big sale, it needs a real person on the other end of the support line - not a ticket queue.

Most of our clients were caught between two bad options: cheap hosts that couldn't handle their traffic, or expensive managed platforms that bundled in services they didn't need. LayerOneGrid is the middle ground we kept wishing existed.

What LayerOneGrid Offers

  • NVMe SSD Storage: Significantly faster read/write speeds than traditional SSDs - critical for database-heavy eCommerce platforms.
  • No Resource Overselling: Your allocated CPU and memory are yours. We don't pack servers beyond their capacity.
  • Redis Caching: In-memory object caching out of the box, so dynamic pages load at static-site speeds.
  • Automated Backups: Daily backups with straightforward restore options, so you're covered before a deployment goes sideways.
  • Staging Environments: Test changes before they go live - standard on every plan, not an upsell.
  • SSL Certificates: Included and auto-renewed. No manual certificate management.
  • Uptime Monitoring: We watch your site around the clock so you don't have to.

Who It's For

LayerOneGrid is built for businesses where downtime has a real cost: eCommerce stores, lead-generation sites, membership platforms, and agencies managing multiple client sites. If your website drives revenue, your hosting infrastructure should be treated accordingly.
